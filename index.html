About Me

Name: Adelegan Abigail Mojolaoluwa

Profession: Executive Assistant

I provide administrative and operational support to executives and department heads. My work focuses on creating systems that improve efficiency, managing documentation, coordinating communications between stakeholders, and handling the details that keep leadership focused on strategic decisions.

Over the past four years, I've supported academic leadership, managed research coordination, handled confidential correspondence, and worked across multiple projects simultaneously. My experience includes supervising undergraduate projects at the University of Uyo, managing social media portfolios for multiple brands at Ennovate Lab, and coordinating field research projects that required stakeholder engagement and data management.

I hold a First Class B.Tech in Agricultural Economics from LAUTECH and I'm currently completing my Master's degree at the University of Uyo. I'm a Graduate Member of the Nigerian Institute of Management (Chartered) and a Registered Blockchain Professional. I continue to develop skills in data science, intellectual property, and emerging technologies.
